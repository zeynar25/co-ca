About Co-Ca

At Co-Ca, our mission is simple yet impactful: to help people across the globe become more familiar with countries, their capital cities, and their rich cultures. We believe that understanding geography is more than just memorizing maps and names; it’s a way to appreciate the diversity and uniqueness of our shared world.

Why Co-Ca?

The world is a vast and beautiful place, home to different nations, cultures, traditions, and histories. By learning about countries and their capitals, we take the first step toward understanding and respecting the unique identities that make each country special. Co-Ca isn’t just a quiz platform; it’s a bridge to global awareness.


  • Fostering Respect for Cultures: At its core, Co-Ca is about more than just knowledge—it’s about building respect. By learning about a country's capital city and its place on the map, you gain insights into its culture, people, and way of life. These small steps can lead to a more connected and compassionate global community.
  • Testing and Expanding Your Knowledge: Think you know all the capital cities in the world? Or perhaps you want to challenge yourself? Co-Ca is here to test your knowledge and push your boundaries. Whether you’re a geography enthusiast, a curious learner, or someone preparing for an important quiz or competition, Co-Ca offers a fun and interactive way to learn.
  • Making Learning Fun and Interactive: Geography can sometimes feel overwhelming, but Co-Ca makes it engaging through gamified learning. Answer questions, test your memory, and track your progress—all while having fun! With challenges ranging from identifying capital cities to matching countries, there’s always something new to learn and enjoy.

How Are Points Calculated?

Co-Ca uses a simple point system to reward your efforts and encourage you to keep learning:

  • Identification: Worth 5 points per correct answer. These questions test your ability to type out answers, making them the most challenging and rewarding.
  • Multiple Choice: Worth 3 points per correct answer. Select the right option from a list of possible answers.
  • True or False: Worth 2 points per correct answer. A quick and simple way to test your knowledge.

This point system ensures that more effortful tasks, like identification, are rewarded accordingly, while quick questions like True or False still help you accumulate points.

Our Vision

We envision a world where learning about geography and cultures becomes second nature—where understanding each other’s differences fosters unity rather than division. Co-Ca aims to empower individuals with knowledge and the ability to connect with people from all walks of life.

How Does It Work?

  • Learn Through Quizzes: Answer questions about countries and their capitals in various formats, including multiple-choice, true or false, and identification.
  • Track Your Progress: Keep track of your scores and see how your knowledge improves over time.
  • Respect and Curiosity: Beyond facts and figures, learn the deeper significance of each country and its culture.

Join Us on This Journey

Whether you’re a student, a trivia enthusiast, or someone who simply loves exploring the world, Co-Ca welcomes you. Together, let’s discover the wonders of the globe, one country and one capital at a time.
